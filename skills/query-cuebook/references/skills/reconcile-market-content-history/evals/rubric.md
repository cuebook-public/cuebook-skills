# Content History Rubric

| Dimension | 5 means |
| --- | --- |
| Publication truth | Attempt, acknowledgement, durable publication, edits, and removal are independently proven |
| Idempotency | Ambiguous creates stop retries and preserve duplicate risk |
| Correction integrity | Every material correction has evidence, public action, approval, and full dependency propagation |
| Outcome separation | Engagement, calibration, and executed trading never share labels or claims |
| Cohort integrity | Denominator, consent, fills, fees, adjustments, exclusions, and restatements are explicit |
| Learning integrity | Cutoffs, windows, features, labels, and forward-time splits prevent hindsight leakage |

Any fabricated receipt, fill, consent, cohort, or correction state is a hard failure.
