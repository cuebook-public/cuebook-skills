# Stable Intake Failure Cases

| Case | Failure | Required response |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Tracking URLs duplicate one article | Source count is inflated | Keep one content cluster and preserve aliases |
| Source updated after cutoff | Later fact leaks into historical replay | Quarantine the later revision |
| Calendar entry says earnings are scheduled | Schedule becomes reported result | Preserve event status and require completion evidence |
| Model narrative says 70% priced in | Hypothesis becomes consensus fact | Keep narrative class and evidence gap |
| Winning idea entered after the move | Backfill looks prospective | Preserve observed and available times |
| Self-reported trade says executed | Fill is treated as reconciled | Keep self-reported state and block performance reuse |
| Position field omitted | Missing becomes flat | Store unknown and require disclosure resolution |
| Source is retracted | Derivative record stays active | Block or quarantine every dependent record |
