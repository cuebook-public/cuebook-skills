# Planning Patterns

## Topology Selection

| Mode | Use when | Minimum structure | Avoid |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `single` | One editorial job and one destination are enough | One item | Artificially multiplying a thin source |
| `anchor_and_derivatives` | A deep source supports one canonical treatment and native derivatives | One anchor and at least one child | Copying the anchor wording into every channel |
| `serial` | Understanding depends on ordered steps | Two or more ordered items | Calling unrelated posts a series |
| `event_lifecycle` | Facts can change through initial report, confirmation, update, and recap | Expiry, update dependencies, correction path | Publishing stale phases as current |
| `community_loop` | The value comes from questions, replies, edits, and synthesis | Discussion item and reply item with named context | Generic cross-community promotion |
| `evergreen_series` | Independent educational units share a durable curriculum | Two or more evergreen items | Smuggling a live call into an educational label |

## Platform Jobs

- X: compression, one sharp observation, thread only when sequence is necessary.
- Telegram: fast context, source links, updates, and durable channel continuity.
- Reddit: named community, self-contained question or analysis, reply and edit plan.
- Xiaohongshu: visual decomposition, one card job at a time, additive caption.
- Douyin: spoken explanation, timed proof, short on-screen text, visible temporal context.
- Long form: assumptions, evidence, countercase, valuation or scenario bridge, sources.
- Owned website: canonical treatment, update and correction path, SEO eligibility, and optional GEO entity/citation sidecar.
- Buy-side note: decision relevance, deltas, watch items, and invalidation.

These are planning defaults, not guarantees of performance. Apply a current `MediaFormatV1` downstream when available.

## Release Strategies

- `single_channel`: one item, one destination.
- `staggered`: independent items spread by relative order or cadence.
- `synchronized`: independent items may release together; dependent items may not.
- `anchor_then_derivatives`: release the canonical item before its children.
- `event_triggered`: release only when an observable condition occurs.

The program stores relative intent. Exact account, timestamp, timezone, execution mode, approval, and idempotency belong to `ReleaseBundleV1`.

## Measurement

Write questions, not targets disguised as certainty.

- Content quality: source corrections, unsupported-claim rate, completion of evidence jobs.
- Distribution: impressions, delivery failures, moderation state, reach by platform.
- Audience response: saves, substantive replies, source contributions, completion rate.
- Market outcome: later asset or thesis observations, kept separate from content quality.

Use fixed windows such as 1 hour, 24 hours, and 7 days only when they fit the channel. Missing metrics stay missing, never zero.
