# Huashu-Adapted Art Direction For Cuebook

This reference adapts useful direction methods from [alchaincyf/huashu-design](https://github.com/alchaincyf/huashu-design), an MIT-licensed design skill. It keeps visible comparison, content-derived form, real-world benchmarks, and rendered critique while adding Cuebook market-data constraints.

## Three Independent Design Logics

Every unresolved viewpoint receives three real HTML previews. Use the same message, source bindings, and canvas for all three.

### 1. Product-Native Editorial Baseline

- Begin with Cuebook's own reading behavior: claim in three seconds, reason second, implication third.
- Use restrained typography, one visual hero, quiet brand signature, and direct labels.
- Treat this as the clarity control, not the intentionally plain option.

### 2. Verified Benchmark Transfer

- Select a real editorial, data-journalism, financial-research, or information-design reference whose composition principle fits the thesis.
- Verify that the reference exists when it is named. Extract one structural idea such as tension, crop, sequencing, scale contrast, or use of negative space.
- Transfer the principle into Cuebook tokens and market semantics. Do not reproduce another brand's logo, palette, copy, or signature composition.
- Record the reference and the transferred principle in working notes; public output does not need a citation badge unless the reference supplies factual content.

### 3. Content-Native Custom Direction

- Derive the form from the thesis's unique relationship: pressure absorbed by a path, two estimates converging, a ladder becoming more fragile, an event opening a valuation gap, or a threshold dividing observed and future time.
- Cover the words and ask whether the visual still points to this specific market argument.
- If the motif can be swapped onto an unrelated ticker, generate a new concept.

## Selection Discipline

- Render before choosing. Text-only style labels are not evidence.
- Keep skeletons structurally different; color swaps do not count.
- Compare at 1244 x 800 and 622 x 400, then verify the 1866 x 1200 publication capture.
- Score concept, three-second comprehension, hierarchy, data integrity, craft, and originality.
- A mixed direction becomes a new rendered direction with its own critique and bindings.

## Cuebook Overrides

- Source fidelity outranks visual drama.
- A verified benchmark may influence form, never market facts.
- Real market series, levels, dates, events, and metrics require bindings.
- Qualitative relationships use qualitative geometry; they cannot borrow quantitative scale.
- No generic AI gradients, floating feature cards, stock imagery, or cinematic brand-film endings.
