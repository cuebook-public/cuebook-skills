# Outcome And Claim Policy

## Separate Planes

| Plane | Valid use | Invalid promotion |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Publication | Prove attempt, acknowledgement, durable publication, edit, correction, retraction, or removal | Treating a prepared bundle or HTTP success as publication |
| Content performance | Improve hook, format, distribution, timing, and audience fit | Validating a factual claim or investment thesis |
| Market calibration | Score a preregistered idea/scenario after its window | Claiming an executed return or track record |
| Trading performance | Evaluate complete authorized broker-reconciled executed cohorts | Including paper, ideas, winner-only uploads, or synthetic fills |

## Public Performance Eligibility

Eligibility requires all of the following:

- record type `executed`
- broker reconciliation
- complete eligible cohort and stated denominator
- explicit public or aggregate consent matching the claim
- complete fills and fee treatment
- documented financing, FX, dividends, and corporate actions when applicable
- benchmark and valuation methodology
- missing-data, exclusion, correction, and restatement policy

An eligible reconciliation still does not draft or approve the claim. It only permits a later governed review.

## Learning

Freeze features at their decision cutoff. Attach outcomes only after a registered window closes. Preserve selected, deferred, rejected, unpublished, and missing-outcome cases. Use forward-time evaluation; random row splits leak future regimes.
