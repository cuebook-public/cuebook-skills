# Evaluation Rubric

- Preserves ResearchPack fact and source IDs.
- Records deterministic input hash, ruleset version, and exact source locators.
- Separates SEO eligibility from answer extractability.
- Resolves entities and aliases without ticker or security conflation.
- Keeps questions observed, derived, or hypothetical.
- Binds every answer unit and direct claim to facts and sources.
- Verifies that referenced research sources actually cover every bound fact ID.
- Distinguishes direct, partial, and unsupported citation support.
- Records current crawler controls without promising retrieval.
- Requires raw evidence and prompt versions for real samples.
- Exposes numerator and denominator for every sample rate.
- Routes issues to a named downstream owner without writing or publishing.
