# Media Format Distillation Rubric

- **Provenance**: every reusable rule resolves to MediaCorpusV1 item IDs.
- **Separation**: format mechanics remain separate from author identity and investment facts.
- **Structure**: sections, cards, beats, packaging, evidence, and interaction are represented when applicable.
- **Sampling**: top-only or mixed samples remain caution; engagement is not treated as truth.
- **Originality**: no signature phrase, exact template, or source asset is exposed for replication.
- **Policy**: current platform and community rules are marked for live recheck.
- **Handoff**: stable media rule IDs can be reported by the renderer.
