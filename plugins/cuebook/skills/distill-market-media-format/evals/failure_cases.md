# Stable Failure Cases

- A single viral post described as a proven formula.
- Reddit distilled without subreddit, flair, OP intent, edits, or comments.
- Video transcript flattened into prose with no beat timing or visual track.
- Xiaohongshu caption returned without cover and card sequence.
- Seeking Alpha structure converted into an AI-written submission.
- Public visibility treated as permission to reuse images or charts.
- A person-level catchphrase emitted as a reusable media rule.
