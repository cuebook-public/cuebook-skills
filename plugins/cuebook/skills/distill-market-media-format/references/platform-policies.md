# Platform Policy Routing Notes

Checked: 2026-07-14. Recheck official policies and target-community rules before a `publish_ready` result. These notes route work; they are not legal advice.

## Seeking Alpha

- Current [Article Submission Guidelines](https://about.seekingalpha.com/article-submission-guidelines) prohibit generative AI from writing, rewriting, or clarifying contributor articles.
- [Editorial Policies](https://about.seekingalpha.com/summary-editorial-policies) require sourced facts, transparent valuation assumptions, balanced risk discussion, and relevant disclosures.
- [Terms of Use](https://about.seekingalpha.com/terms) restrict copying, derivative reuse, automated collection, and redistribution.

Allowed use here: analyze public structure, build a fact ledger, or create an internal outline. Block AI-generated or AI-edited copy labeled as a Seeking Alpha submission.

## Reddit

- [Reddit Rules](https://redditinc.com/policies/reddit-rules) and the [Spam policy](https://support.reddithelp.com/hc/en-us/articles/360043504051-Spam) prohibit manipulation and repeated unsolicited promotion.
- Every subreddit has local rules. For example, [r/investing rules](https://www.reddit.com/r/investing/wiki/index/rules/) specify its own DD and source requirements.
- [Reddit's User Agreement](https://redditinc.com/policies/user-agreement) and [Data API Terms](https://redditinc.com/policies/data-api-terms) govern content rights and automated access.

Require a current rules URL and check time for the named community. Keep affiliation transparent, draft for the actual thread, and never treat votes as evidence quality.

## Xiaohongshu

- Current finance-account governance is time-sensitive. The platform's [professional account qualification help](https://ad.xiaohongshu.com/next_help/docs/195c5fe505c71b4b0335a2fe0d61d8e0) shows that finance-related identities and commercial activity can require qualification.
- A June 2026 governance update appears in the platform's [`薯管家` announcement](https://weibo.com/5859555080/R2nBKlHlU) and was reported by [中新经纬](https://www.jwview.com/jingwei/html/m/06-04/673518.shtml). Verify the live official account notice before publishing.

Record account qualification, content identity, commercial relationship, asset rights, and disclosure. Qualification unknown means conditional for professional investment analysis or marketing. Personalized orders remain blocked.

## Douyin

- Douyin published a trial finance-industry community convention in December 2025; see the [official convention text](https://95152.douyin.com/article/5561765854302017) and contemporaneous reporting by [中国金融信息网](https://www.cnfin.com/gs-lb/detail/20251216/4351490_1.html).
- The convention and related governance distinguish financial education from unqualified professional advice and misleading marketing. Verify the current official text and account qualification before publishing.

Record qualification and content class. Block personalized orders, direct position sizing, leverage instructions, and unsupported buy/sell levels. Use visible disclosures for financial relationships and uncertainty.

## China Financial Information Baseline

- The current [Financial Information Services rules](https://www.cac.gov.cn/2018-12/26/c_1123908386.htm) require attributable, traceable financial information and prohibit false or distorted financial information.
- The [Measures for Online Marketing of Financial Products](https://www.cac.gov.cn/2026-04/24/c_1778769008779432.htm) are scheduled to take effect on 2026-09-30. They are not yet effective as of this snapshot; refresh the legal routing when the effective date arrives.
