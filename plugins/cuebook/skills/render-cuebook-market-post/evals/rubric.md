# Market Post Rubric

Score each dimension from 1 to 5. Any hard failure blocks publication regardless of average score.

| Dimension | 5 means |
| --- | --- |
| Projection integrity | Source, asset, direction, directness, and proxy chain all agree |
| Evidence and freshness | Every hard claim is traceable; current facts are timestamped |
| Comparator and tape discipline | Period, basis, consensus, prior, event window, and benchmark are aligned where relevant |
| Market reasoning | The post explains who may act, why, and what changes next |
| Angle quality | One supported tension carries the post without forced drama |
| Human language | Concrete, conversational, varied, and free of template residue |
| Frame fit | The title, reasoned three-to-five-paragraph body, and paired image divide labor without platform wrappers, padding, or duplicated sections |
| Decision utility | A reader can follow the asset expression, horizon, market consequence, and next observable |
| Assisted authorship | Internal provenance keeps the creator seed, Cue/evidence-linked proposals, acceptance or rejection, creator judgment, and idea change distinct while only adopted additions enter meaning and body copy contains no workflow narration |
| Creator lift | The creator's idea is recognizably theirs and feels clearer, sharper, and better expressed rather than graded or corrected by default |
| Lineage integrity | The output resolves to selected opportunities, its program item, and every input artifact |

Publication thresholds:

- `ready`: no hard failures; projection and evidence both 5; all other dimensions at least 4.
- `conditional`: no hard failures; projection and evidence at least 4; uncertainty is explicit.
- `blocked`: any hard failure, unsupported material number, wrong asset, or unverified current claim.

Hard failures:

- source-to-asset mismatch
- direction conflict
- missing proxy bridge
- fabricated source, quote, current price, or number
- living-author impersonation
- public draft emitted after a rejected gate or blocked research decision
- public draft emitted after RouteV1 abstains or the policy gate blocks
- research pack reference with no carried quality decision
- missing selected-opportunity, program-item, or input-artifact lineage
- ready state with unknown position or commercial disclosure status
- personalized order quantity, sizing, leverage, credential, or execution instruction
- fabricated creator seed or unsupported claim that Cuebook discovered or improved an idea
- public narration of Cuebook assistance, idea completion, accepted additions, or authoring workflow
- self-correction headings such as `我认错` or `什么情况算看错`
- social-platform variants, labels, or wrappers in the public result
- a public result with visible fields beyond title, body, and one paired image
- correction-first rewriting of a creator-owned viewpoint without a material evidence contradiction
- a Cue treated as proof, consensus, or current-creator ownership without explicit adoption
- a clearly framed creator hypothesis blocked solely because the inference itself lacks direct proof
